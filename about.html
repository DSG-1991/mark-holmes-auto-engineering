Our Story: Built on Trust, Driven by Quality

Mark Holmes Auto Engineering opened its doors in 1993 with a simple mission: provide honest, high-quality car repairs that Fleetwood residents could rely on. Over three decades later, that mission hasn't changed.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into a well-known fixture in the local community. We've earned our reputation one job at a time — through fair pricing, skilled workmanship, and a genuine commitment to doing right by our customers.

We're a small, independent garage, and that's exactly how we like it. It means every customer gets personal attention. You'll deal directly with the people working on your car. No call centres, no sales pressure — just straightforward, expert service.

We've been serving Fleetwood since 1993 — that's over 30 years of keeping local cars, vans, and families moving. As a DVSA-approved MOT test centre, we also welcome drivers from Cleveleys, Thornton-Cleveleys, Poulton-le-Fylde and across the wider Fylde coast. We're proud to be part of this community and look forward to serving it for decades to come.
