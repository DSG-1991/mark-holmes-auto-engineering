Our Services

Everything your vehicle needs, under one roof. From MOTs to major repairs — we've got you covered.

MOT Testing

DVSA-approved Class 4 MOT tests carried out by experienced, professional testers at our Fleetwood test centre. We'll give your vehicle a thorough inspection and provide clear, honest advice on any work needed to pass. No unnecessary repairs — just straightforward service you can trust.

We recommend booking your MOT at least a week in advance, especially during peak seasons. If your vehicle fails, we'll explain exactly what's needed and can carry out repairs to get you back on the road quickly.

Book Your MOT →

Servicing & Maintenance

Regular servicing is the best way to protect your vehicle's value and reliability. We offer interim, full, and major services tailored to your car's age, mileage, and manufacturer recommendations.

Every service includes a fresh oil and filter change, fluid top-ups, a comprehensive multi-point inspection, and a detailed report. We'll flag any developing issues before they become expensive problems.

Book a Service →

Diagnostics

Modern cars are complex machines. When a warning light appears or something doesn't feel right, our professional diagnostic equipment quickly pinpoints the issue. We read fault codes, interpret live data, and trace problems to their source.

From check engine lights and ABS faults to transmission issues and electrical gremlins — we diagnose accurately so we fix right first time, saving you time and money.

Book a Diagnostic →

Brake Repairs

Your brakes are your car's most important safety system. We carry out all brake work — pad and disc replacement, caliper repairs, brake fluid flushes, and handbrake adjustments.

We inspect your braking system thoroughly and give you a clear picture of what's needed. If you hear squealing, feel vibration through the pedal, or notice a longer stopping distance, don't wait — bring it in.

Book Brake Repairs →

Air Conditioning

Is your air conditioning not blowing cold? We offer full AC services including re-gassing, system pressure checks, leak detection, and component repairs. Regular AC maintenance keeps the system efficient and prevents costly failures.

We recommend having your AC serviced every 1-2 years to maintain performance and prevent compressor damage. A well-maintained system also helps demist your windscreen faster in winter.

Book AC Service →

Tyres & 4 Wheel Alignment

We supply and fit tyres from leading brands at competitive prices. Whether you need one replacement or a full set, we'll help you choose the right tyre for your driving style and budget.

Our 4-wheel alignment service corrects tracking and camber angles, reducing uneven tyre wear, improving fuel economy, and giving you sharper handling. Wheel balancing is included with every tyre fitting.

Book Tyre Service →

Exhaust Repairs

A noisy or leaking exhaust isn't just annoying — it can affect your car's performance and fuel economy, and it will fail an MOT. We carry out exhaust repairs and replacements for all vehicle types.

From a small patch repair to a full system replacement, we'll advise on the most cost-effective solution. We use quality components that last, and all work is fully guaranteed.

Book Exhaust Repair →

General Repairs

From suspension work and steering components to belts, hoses, and cooling systems — we handle all types of car repairs. No job is too big or too small for our experienced team.

We work on most makes and models, from small hatchbacks to family SUVs and work vans. Every repair comes with honest advice, transparent pricing, and a guarantee on parts and labour.

Book a Repair →

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for drivers across Fleetwood and the Fylde coast.

How much is an MOT in Fleetwood?

An MOT test at Mark Holmes Auto Engineering is £48.00. A Pre-MOT check is £10.00, and we also offer an MOT combined with an interim, full or major service. Call 01253 878932 or book online.

Are you a DVSA-approved MOT test centre?

Yes. We're a DVSA-approved Class 4 MOT testing station in Fleetwood, carrying out MOTs on cars and small vans with experienced, professional testers.

What are your opening hours?

We're open Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and bank holidays.

Where are you based and which areas do you cover?

You'll find us at Unit 6 Kilbane Street, Fleetwood, FY7 7PF. As well as Fleetwood, we look after drivers from Cleveleys, Thornton-Cleveleys, Poulton-le-Fylde and across the wider Fylde coast.

How do I book an appointment?

Book online using the form on this page, or call us on 01253 878932 and we'll find a time that works for you. For MOTs we recommend booking at least a week in advance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, debit cards and credit cards. Payment is due on completion of the work, and we provide a full itemised invoice for all services and repairs.
